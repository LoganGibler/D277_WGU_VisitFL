Tallahassee Information

Tallahassee, the vibrant capital of Florida, offers a unique blend of rich history, stunning natural beauty, and a lively cultural scene, making it a must-visit destination. Nestled among rolling hills and lush greenery, the city is home to picturesque parks, such as Alfred B. Maclay Gardens State Park, where visitors can enjoy scenic walking trails and beautiful gardens. History enthusiasts will appreciate the opportunity to explore landmarks like the Florida State Capitol and the Museum of Florida History, which provide insights into the state's past. With its friendly atmosphere, delicious Southern cuisine, and a calendar full of events and festivals, Tallahassee invites visitors to experience the charm and hospitality of the Deep South. Whether you're looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or simply a relaxing getaway, Tallahassee is a destination that promises to delight.

Activities

  • Explore the Florida State Capitol: Tour the historic building and enjoy views from the observation deck.
  • Visit the Museum of Florida History: Discover exhibits on Florida's diverse history and culture.
  • Stroll through Alfred B. Maclay Gardens State Park: Enjoy beautiful gardens, hiking trails, and scenic views.
  • Hike at the Tallahassee-St. Marks Historic Railroad State Trail: Experience the picturesque 20-mile trail perfect for walking and biking.
  • Relax at Cascades Park: Enjoy the amphitheater, playgrounds, walking paths, and the stunning waterfall.
  • Discover the Florida State University Campus: Walk around the campus and visit the FSU Museum of Fine Arts.
